Ingredients:

- 1 cup of grinded and fried cassava (garri)
- 3 cubs of sugar
- 3 spoons of powdered milk
- 1/2 cup of groundnut
- 1 litre of water

Directions:

1. In a large bowl, add the sugar, garri and pour some amount of water so that the
	the content is soaked.

2. Add the milk stir properly until all milk particles have dissolved in the mixture.

3	Pour a handfull of groundnut into the mixture and serve cool.
